About Me

Welcome to Photo_RBP Business! My name is Ruben, and I am passionate about capturing life's most precious moments. My journey into photography began when I started playing around with a small digital camera. I noticed that every time I took a photo, it brought me joy when I looked back at it days, months, or even years later. Inspired by this, I decided to save up for a decent professional camera to capture more of life's special moments. Initially, I focused on photographing nature and cars, but eventually, I transitioned to capturing images of people. I attended community events, parties, and weddings, finding immense joy in documenting these occasions. Each shot I take represents a cherished memory, a special moment that I aim to make unforgettable for you.
